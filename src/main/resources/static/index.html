🔬 Chip Industry Primer

Fabless vs IDM: Fabless companies (NVDA, AMD) have higher margins but depend on TSMC. IDMs (INTC, TXN) have more control but higher capex.
The AI Supercycle: AI training needs GPUs (NVDA), memory (MU HBM), power chips (MPWR), networking (AVGO, MRVL). Data-centre chip demand growing 30-50%/yr.
EV Tailwind: Each EV uses 2-5× more chips than a petrol car. SiC power chips (ON, STM) and MCUs (MCHP, TXN) are key beneficiaries.

ETFs

One trade, instant diversification. Expense ratio is everything for passive funds.

Expense Ratio: Annual fee. 0.03% = $3/yr per $10k. Always choose lower cost when tracking the same index.
AUM: Total $ invested. Larger = more liquid = tighter spreads.
Distribution Yield: Income paid out annually. Different from total return (which includes price appreciation).

Dividends

Income investing — get paid while you wait for price appreciation.

Dividend King: 50+ consecutive years of increases. ~50 companies qualify worldwide.
Aristocrat: 25+ years of increases AND in the S&P 500. ~65 companies.
Payout Ratio: % of earnings paid as dividends. Under 60% = healthy. Over 80% = risky.
DRIP: Reinvest dividends to buy more shares automatically. Massively accelerates compounding.
